About Ranked Royale

Ranked Royale is an independent community platform designed to bring a high-stakes, competitive edge to the Clash Royale experience. We provide a custom ELO ranking system that allows players to track their progress, compete in organized tournaments, and find teammates in a dedicated environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a fair and competitive environment for players of all skill levels. Whether you are a casual player looking to improve or a professional looking for a challenge, Ranked Royale provides the tools you need to succeed.

Who We Are

We are a small team of passionate developers and gamers who felt that the existing ladder systems could be enhanced with better matchmaking and social features. Ranked Royale is a project under Room310 Holdings.

Disclaimer: Ranked Royale is an unofficial community site. We are not affiliated with, endorsed, sponsored, or specifically approved by Supercell. For more information, please refer to Supercell's Fan Content Policy.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required to provide our services, including:

  • Email address for account creation and security.
  • In-game player tags to sync match history and rankings.
  • Profile information you choose to provide (avatar, bio).

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used to calculate ELO rankings, manage tournament brackets, and allow other users to find you via the friend system. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (via Supabase) to protect your account data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize Supercell's public API to retrieve match data. By using our site, you acknowledge that your public game statistics are being accessed through these official channels.

5. Safety Warning

We will NEVER ask you for your Email Password, Supercell Login, Banking Information, or highly personal details (Address, Age, etc). If you are asked for these by someone claiming to be Ranked Royale staff, THEY ARE AN IMPOSTER. PLEASE ALERT US IMMEDIATELY.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about your data, have found a bug, or wish to delete your account, please contact us via the Support page.
